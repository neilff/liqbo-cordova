About Liqbo

Liqbo is a hybrid mobile application built on top of the Ionic HTML5 Framework. It is developed and maintained by Neil Fenton

Liqbo is powered by the LCBO API developed by Carsten Nielsen

Liqbo is not affiliated with the LCBO

Find Products

To search for products, use the search bar in the header. Enter your query and press submit to view details for up to 20 products.

Tag a Product as Favourite

To add a product to your favourite list, navigate to the product details page, then click Favourite.

To view your favourite products, navigate back to the drink search page and reveal the menu. Favourites will appear at the bottom of the Options menu.

Find Local Stores

To search for stores near your current location, press the navigation button to find local stores.

Perform a Store Search

To search for a specific store using a query, press the menu button to reveal the search engine.

When you save stores as favourites, you will find them here.
